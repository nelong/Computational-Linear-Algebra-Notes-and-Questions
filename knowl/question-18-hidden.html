  1. What is the average speed over \([0,2 \pi]\text{?}\)
  2. What is the speed at \(t=2\pi\text{?}\)
  3. What is the speed at \(t=0\text{?}\)
  4. What is the velocity at \(t=2\pi\text{?}\)
  5. What is the velocity at \(t=0\text{?}\)
  6. What is the length of the curve \(C\text{?}\)
