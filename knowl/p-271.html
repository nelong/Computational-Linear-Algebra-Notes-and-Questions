Let \(A\) be a \(n\) by \(n\) matrix. The following are equivalent:
  1. \(A\) is an invertible matrix.
  2. \(A\) is row equivalent to \(Id_n\text{.}\)
  3. \(A\) has \(n\) pivots.
  4. \(A\vec{x} =\vec{0}\) has only the trivial solution.
  5. The linear transformation \(\vec{x} \rightarrow A\vec{x}\) is one-to-one.
  6. The linear transformation \(\vec{x} \rightarrow A\vec{x}\) is onto.
  7. \(A\vec{x}=\vec{b}\) has a solution for every \(\vec{b} \in \mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\)
  8. The columns of \(A\) form a linearly independent set.
  9. The columns of \(A\) span \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\)
  10. The rows of \(A\) form a linearly independent set.
  11. The rows of \(A\) span \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\)
  12. \(A^T\) is invertible.
